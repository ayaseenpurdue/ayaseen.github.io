Cooking Cabin Services



Here at the Cooking Cabin, we offer a multitude of services in the form of cooking classes! Choose from a wide variety of teachers, who specialized in different techniques and ethnicities of food. Here, you can learn from our skilled staff how to be a better cook.


One-Session Classes

Take one of our one-session classes to spend a handful of hours learning something specific. These classes are by commission, so you get a personal session with our professional staff. The ingredients and utensils required will be provided, but you are welcome to bring your own! See below for the classes offered and their pricing:

  • Italian Dinner - 3 hours - $122: learn how to make two classic italian dishes
  • Dessert Trio - 4 hours - $148: spend four hours learning how to make a few treats, and finish the night by freestyling your own dessert!
  • Lunch Special - 2 hours - $90: find out how to make a balanced lunch that you’ll never get tired of

Weeklong Program - $889

Our weekly sessions run for an entire week, spanning 5 hours each day. In these, you will go on a deep dive into cooking. You’ll go beyond just understanding how to make dishes, but to be able to design your own courses of meals that pair well together. This service is very popular and hard to ensure a spot, so when they are available, click here to contact us right away. The weeklong program has a maximum size of 12.

  1. Monday - Introduction to the kitchen
  2. Tuesday - Learning the ins and outs of breakfast
  3. Wednesday - Making a balanced lunch
  4. Thursday - The Big Dinner
  5. Friday - Bringing it all together
  6. Saturday - the culminating event where you put what you’ve learned together to make a course.
